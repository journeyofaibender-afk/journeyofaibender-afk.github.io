AI Helper Starter Kit

A free copy-paste prompt pack by @journeyofaibender. Share freely — just credit. 💚

How to use: open ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude, paste a prompt, fill the [brackets], hit enter. That's it. No tech skills required. Tap Copy on any prompt.

1) Everyday Help

"Act as a friendly assistant. Help me write a clear [email / text / message] to [person] about [topic]. Keep it warm and simple."
"Summarize this like I'm 10 years old: [paste text]."
"Fix the grammar and tone of this so it sounds confident but kind: [paste text]."
"Give me 5 ideas for [occasion, e.g. my mom's birthday] that I can do on a [budget]."

2) Small Business / Side Hustle

"I run a [type of business]. Write 3 Instagram captions that sound like me and mention [product/service]."
"Write a friendly auto-reply for my DMs that answers: hours, where to order, and how to pay."
"Turn my last 5 sold-out products into a short, punchy bio for my profile."
"Give me 10 post ideas for a [niche] business that don't feel salesy."

3) For Non-Techies / Learners

"Act as a patient teacher. Explain [topic] in plain language with one real-life example."
"Quiz me on [subject] with 5 easy questions, then explain any I get wrong."
"Help me write a [resume / cover letter] for a [role]. Ask me 3 questions first."
"I'm overwhelmed. Break [task] into 5 tiny steps I can do today."

4) Accessibility & Care

"Describe this photo out loud in detail for someone who can't see it: [describe or attach]."
"Turn this voice note into a clean written message: [paste]."
"Make a simple daily routine checklist for [person/condition] that's easy to follow."
"Explain this medical/legal doc in plain words (not advice): [paste]."

5) Creative & Joy

"Write lyrics for a short song about [theme] in a [happy / calm] mood."
"Help me write a heartfelt caption for a photo of [moment]."
"Plan a 1-hour fun activity for [kids / friends / family] with things we have at home."

Rules of Thumb

  • WHO + WHAT: tell the AI who to be and what to do.
  • One task per prompt.
  • If it's wrong, say "try again, make it [shorter / friendlier / simpler]."
  • Never share passwords or private info with any AI.
