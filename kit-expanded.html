Everyday & Productivity

Plan my day
I have these tasks today: [list them]. I get easily distracted. Give me a realistic hourly schedule with breaks, and clearly flag the 2 most important tasks.
Inbox zero in 10 minutes
Here are my unread emails: [paste them]. For each, tell me: reply now / delete / file away / needs action later. Draft a short reply for the reply-now ones.
Weekly meal plan
Plan 5 simple dinners for the week. Constraints: [diet needs, e.g. no pork / vegetarian], budget around [amount], under 30 minutes each. Include a shopping list.
20-minute room reset
My [bedroom/living room] is a mess and I keep avoiding it. Give me a step-by-step 20-minute cleanup plan I can follow without overthinking.

Writing & Communication

Rewrite it nicely
Rewrite this message to be polite but firm. Keep it under 3 sentences: [paste your message].
Reply to something awkward
Someone sent me this: [paste]. I want to [decline / set a boundary / stay friendly]. Draft 2 different replies I could send.
Fix my grammar & tone
Correct the grammar and improve the tone of this: [paste]. First show only the changes, then give the cleaned-up version.
Summarize for a 12-year-old
Summarize this in 5 bullet points that a 12-year-old could understand: [paste].

Learning & Study

Explain like I'm 12
Explain [topic] like I'm 12 years old, using a real-life analogy. No jargon.
Build me a study plan
I have [X days] to learn [topic] from zero. Make a daily plan with what to watch, read, and do each day.
Quiz me (don't give answers)
Quiz me on [topic] with 5 questions from easy to hard. Don't give the answers yet — wait for my replies, then mark me and explain the misses.
Notes to flashcards
Turn these notes into question-and-answer flashcards: [paste your notes].

Money & Side Hustle

Budget my income
I earn [amount] per [month/week]. My fixed costs are: [list]. Make me a simple budget that pays savings first.
Find my side hustle
My skills: [list]. Free time: [X hours/week]. Suggest 3 realistic side hustles I can start with zero money, ranked by ease.
Negotiate my bill down
Draft a short message to [internet/phone provider] asking to lower my bill. Mention I'm a loyal customer and considering switching.
Price my service
I offer [describe your service]. Competitors charge [range]. Given my experience level [beginner/intermediate/pro], suggest a fair price and a one-line pitch.

Health & Wellness (non-medical)

30-minute home workout
Give me a no-equipment 30-minute workout for [beginner/intermediate], including a warm-up and cool-down.
Sleep better tonight
I sleep badly. Give me a 1-hour pre-sleep routine and 3 things I should stop doing before bed.
Calm my anxiety now
I'm feeling anxious about [situation]. Walk me through a 5-minute grounding exercise, step by step.
Healthy 5-minute snacks
Suggest 10 healthy snacks I can prep in under 5 minutes with no cooking.

Home & Family

Explain it to my kid
Explain [topic, e.g. why the sky is blue] to my [age]-year-old simply and in a fun way.
Dinner-table questions
Give me 5 conversation questions for family dinner that aren't 'how was your day'.
Cheap birthday at home
Plan a memorable [kid/adult] birthday at home under [budget]. Include a simple activity and an easy cake alternative.
Fair chore chart
Make a fair weekly chore chart for a family of [N] (ages [list]) with rotating tasks.

Creativity & Fun

Story starters
Give me 3 opening lines for a short story in the [fantasy/mystery] genre, set in [place].
Caption my photo
Write 5 witty Instagram captions for a photo of [describe the photo]. Tone: [funny/relatable].
Plan a date night
Plan a [free/cheap] at-home date night for [occasion], with a simple timeline.
Gift ideas
Gift idea for [person, age, relationship, interest], budget [amount]. Give 5 ideas, at least 2 handmade.

Work & Career

Rewrite my CV bullet
Turn this job duty into an achievement bullet that includes a number: [paste]. Give me 3 versions.
Prep for my interview
I have an interview for [role] at [company]. Give me 5 likely questions and a strong answer framework for each.
Polite follow-up
It's been [X days] since I applied / met [person]. Draft a short, polite follow-up message.
Write a strong 'no'
Help me write a polite 'no' to [request] that protects my time but keeps the relationship warm.

Tech for Non-Techies

Explain this error
I got this error message: [paste or describe]. In plain English, what does it mean and what's the simplest fix?
Set up my phone
I'm not techy. Give me 5 settings to change on my [iPhone/Android] to save battery, improve privacy, and reduce annoyance.
Compare before I buy
I'm choosing between [option A] and [option B] for [use case]. Make a simple pros/cons table and a clear recommendation.
Safe online basics
Give me 6 non-techy habits to stay safe online (passwords, scams, wifi) explained simply.

Decisions & Life

Pros & cons, fast
I'm deciding [decision]. List the pros and cons, then tell me what to watch out for either way.
What should I ask myself
I'm stuck on [problem]. Give me 5 questions to ask myself that will clarify the right move.
Reframe a bad day
I had a rough day because [what happened]. Help me reframe it into 3 things I can take away or learn.
Plan a small goal
I want to [goal, e.g. read more]. Break it into a tiny daily version so small I can't fail, plus a 14-day tracker.
